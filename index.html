Privacy Policy for NearLink

Last updated: December 2025

We operates the NearLink mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using NearLink, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use NearLink, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • User-generated content such as messages and tasks

1.2 Location Information

NearLink is a location-based application. With your permission, we may collect:

  • Approximate location
  • Precise location

Location data is used strictly to:

  • Show nearby users
  • Enable nearby chats and tasks
  • Improve app functionality

We do not sell or use your location data for advertising purposes.

1.3 Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash logs and performance data

This data helps us improve app stability, performance, and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable nearby user discovery
  • Allow chat and task interactions
  • Send notifications related to messages, tasks, or updates
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Maintain safety, security, and prevent misuse

3. Notifications

NearLink uses push notifications to notify users about:

  • New messages
  • Nearby tasks or important updates

You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With trusted third-party services required for app functionality
  • When required by law or legal obligations
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the app

5. Third-Party Services

NearLink uses third-party services that may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Firebase Analytics

These services are used solely for authentication, notifications, analytics, and app functionality.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure authentication methods
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Restricted access to databases

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • It is necessary to provide our services

You may request deletion of your account at any time.

8. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion:

  • By contacting us via email

After account deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

9. Children’s Privacy

NearLink is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. User Controls and Choices

You can:

  • Edit or update your profile information
  • Control location permissions through device settings
  • Disable notifications
  • Request account deletion

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
